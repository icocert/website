The Future Waits For No One

ICOcert is a group of serial entrepreneurs, security researchers and experienced developers who believes that cryptocurrencies are here to stay and will disrupt the future in ways never imagined.

The majority of the cryptocurrencies and blockchain-baked projects that are present in the current market were launched as ICO. Their popularity grew over time and so did their number. With respect to the venture capitalists long-established approach, ICO due diligence is significantly harder.

Our mission is to set a standard for ICO assessments with our auditing system that combines information security techniques, open-source intelligence, computer forensics and business risk research in a reliable evaluation.
